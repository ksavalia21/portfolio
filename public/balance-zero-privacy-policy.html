Privacy Policy

Balance Zero  ·  Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

Balance Zero is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. This policy explains that commitment in full.

Data We Collect

None. Balance Zero does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers.

How Your Data Is Stored

All information you enter — card balances, item prices, saved lists, and calculation history — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Balance Zero does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. There are no external network requests of any kind.

Device Permissions

Balance Zero does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or any other sensitive device capability.

Children's Privacy

Balance Zero does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
